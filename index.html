RelicPath

A metal detecting companion app for tracking detecting trips, logging finds, saving GPS routes, reviewing hunt history, exporting records, and managing optional cloud backup features.

About RelicPath

RelicPath helps metal detecting hobbyists track detecting trips, save GPS routes, log finds, attach photo references, review past hunts, view maps, export records, and manage profile and cloud backup features.

The app is designed for responsible hobby use. Users are responsible for obtaining permission to detect, following local laws, protecting private property information, and safely handling recovered items.
