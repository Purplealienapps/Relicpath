What Support Can Help With

Contact RelicPath support for help with:

  • Account sign-in or email verification.
  • Cloud backup and restore questions.
  • RelicPath Pro subscription questions.
  • Trip, find, route, or export issues.
  • Privacy questions.
  • Account and cloud data deletion requests.
  • Bug reports or technical problems.

Before Contacting Support

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • Your RelicPath account email, if applicable.
  • Your device model.
  • Your Android version, if known.
  • A short description of what happened.
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, if possible.
  • Screenshots, if helpful and safe to share.

Do not send sensitive detecting locations, private landowner details, or exact find locations unless they are necessary for the support request.
