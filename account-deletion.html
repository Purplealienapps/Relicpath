RelicPath Account and Data Deletion

Request deletion of your account and associated cloud data.

Overview

RelicPath users can request deletion of their account and associated cloud-stored data. This page explains how to submit a deletion request and what data may be deleted.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your RelicPath account and associated cloud backup data, send an email to:

YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL_HERE

Use this subject line:

RelicPath Account Deletion Request

Include the email address associated with your RelicPath account so the account can be identified.

What Data May Be Deleted

When a valid account deletion request is processed, RelicPath may delete cloud-stored data associated with the account, including:

  • RelicPath account profile information.
  • Privacy settings.
  • Cloud-backed trip records.
  • Cloud-backed find records.
  • Cloud-backed routes and route points.
  • Cloud backup and restore records associated with the account.

Local Device Data

Some RelicPath data may be stored only on your device. RelicPath cannot remotely delete local-only data from a device it cannot access.

To remove local data from your device, you can delete records inside the app where available, uninstall the app, or clear the app’s storage from your Android device settings.

Subscription Data

RelicPath Pro subscriptions are managed through Google Play. Deleting your RelicPath account does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription.

To manage or cancel a subscription, use Google Play subscription settings on your device or Google account.

Data That May Be Retained

Some limited information may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute resolution, or app integrity purposes. Any retained information will be limited to what is reasonably necessary for those purposes.

Processing Time

Account and data deletion requests are generally reviewed and processed as soon as reasonably possible. You may be contacted for verification before deletion is completed.
